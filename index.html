Meet the Owner

Warren Solon,
Hotel Proprietor

Born and raised in Cebu, Warren Solon has always believed that true luxury is not about extravagance — it's about thoughtful details, genuine warmth, and making every guest feel completely at home.

After studying Hospitality Management and spending years honing his craft in international hotels, Warren returned to Cebu with one vision: to build a hotel that combines world-class standards with authentic Filipino soul.
